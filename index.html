National (only for Bangladeshi nationals) employment opportunities

Posted on : May 21, 2024

Deadline: June 20, 2024

Call for Expression of Interest (EOI) – Short Term (Daily Wage) basis services at icddr,b (DW#51/2021)

icddr,b is a Bangladesh-based international health research institute that strives to solve key public health problems through high-quality scientific research and innovation. Icddr,b as a values-based organization encourages women and people with disabilities. The primary objective of the project, which is under the Programme for Emerging Infections, is to identify the precise cause of death of stillbirths and children under five, and to perform mortality surveillance across different hospitals in the Faridpur and Rajbari districts. The project is generating high-quality data in collaboration with the Government of Bangladesh and engaging various government and non-government stakeholders. This evidence-based approach will ultimately help improve maternal and child health by data to action activities. The Human Resources Department of icddr,b is seeking expressions of interest (EOI) from the qualified candidates to meet it’s adhoc basis temporary requirements with relevant experiences in various fields of expertise as follows:

  1. Central Management Services;
  2. Climate Change and Health;
  3. Clinical Services;
  4. Maternal and Child Nutrition;
  5. Maternal and Neonatal Health;
  6. Non-Communicable Diseases;
  7. Support Services;
  8. Training and Education;
  9. Universal Health Coverage;

Objective:

This Traineeship will provide an opportunity for aspiring researchers to work with the eminent public health researchers and receive hands-on training, exposure, experience in a research based environment like icddr,b. The Traineeship programme is designed to enhance the knowledge, skills and performance of the individuals who wants to become an independent researcher at icddr,b. The Traineeship is aimed at capable individuals who have recently completed their Masters or primary professional education, are in the career formative stage (within 3 years of receiving most recent degree), and are interested to make a career in research. The Trainee will be required to perform duties as assigned by the supervisor.

Key Responsibilities:

The Research Trainee will be reporting to the PI/Co-PI or her/his delegate. The Trainee is expected to contribute in public health research under the guidance of the PI. She/he will assist PI and be primarily responsible for the following duties and responsibilities:

  1. Conduct literature review;
  2. Analyze data;
  3. Draft concept notes, protocols, reports and manuscript;
  4. Prepare and deliver presentations;
  5. Support in project coordination and implementation;
  6. Assist in budgeting and financial management.

Required Academic qualification and experience:

  1. Bachelor's Degree in Political Science or social Science background would be preferred.
  2. Fresh graduate will encourage to apply
  3. At least 1 years of experience in relevant field;
  4. Candidate having previous experience in Verbal Autopsy interview, will be given preference;
  5. Must have good typing speed in Bangla.

No of post: 10

Location: Tongi, Gazipur

Stipend & Benefits:

  1. Tk.30,620/- per month (consolidated) inclusive of house rent and conveyance allowances;
  2. The progress of the Research Trainee will be monitored and evaluated. On successful completion of training and final evaluation, the Trainee will be awarded certificates;
  3. Income tax paid by icddr,b and subsidized canteen;

Stipend & Benefits:

After completion of at least 1 (one) year Traineeship, Trainees will be eligible to apply for any other suitable posts against icddr,b vacancy announcements. However, such posts are not guaranteed. The selected Trainee will not be eligible for any other Traineeship programme at the Centre.

Notes of attention:

  1. Only short-listed candidates shall be invited for written test /interview;
  2. cddr,b does not charge any fee at any stage of the recruitment process;
  3. icddr,b encourages women and people with disabilities to apply.

Interested candidates who fulfil the requirements should submit online applications using icddr,b website: career.icddrb.org by June 18, 2024.
